Delete Account - Berjuang To Do List

If you would like to delete your account and associated data from Berjuang To Do List, please follow the steps below:

How to request account deletion

Send an email to [EMAIL KAMU] with the subject "Delete Account Request", including the email address used to register your account.

Data that will be deleted

  • Account information (name, email)
  • Task list and reminders
  • App preferences

Retention period

Your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days of your request.
